Who We Are

We are a team of three web development students at Howest University in Belgium. Our names are Bjarni Heselmans, Emiel Mangelschots, and Lucas Reynders. We are currently working on a project for our Webtechnologie course.

Our Project

Our project is a web application that measures and displays the sound level in decibels. We are using the Web Audio API to capture audio input from the user's microphone and calculate the sound level in real-time. We are also using the W3.CSS framework to style our web application and make it responsive.

Our Goals

Our goal is to create a user-friendly and accurate web application that can be used to measure sound levels in various environments. We hope that our application can help people make informed decisions about noise exposure and protect their hearing.
